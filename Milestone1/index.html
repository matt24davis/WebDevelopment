Welcome

Thank you for visiting the website of the Center for Applied Research in Information Technology (CARIT). CARIT's mission is to serve regional businesses and communities by doing technological research that is results-driven. For KSU graduate and undergraduate students, CARIT will offer a setting that focuses on fostering an applied learning environment with practical research experience in the areas of data collection, data management, and analysis. Please visit the website to learn more about the projects that CARIT has completed or is currently working on. Additionally, you can learn more about the services offered by CARIT.
